PROJECT TITLE: Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze retail business performance and support data-driven decision making.

KEY FEATURES:
- Executive KPI tracking (Sales, Profit, Orders, Profit Margin)
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Category and regional performance breakdown
- Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering
- Top product performance analysis

BUSINESS VALUE:
This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and improve operational decisions.

TOOLS & SKILLS:
Power BI Desktop
DAX Measures
Power Query Data Transformation
Data Visualization
Business Analytics
